How To Play

  • At the start of the game you will need to grab your gun.
  • Use the arrow keys to move around and the space-bar to shoot (after you have gotten the gun).
  • When you get hit by a zombie you lose health and a point off your score per hit,
    you will slowly regenerate your health over time.
  • When you kill a zombie you gain the amount of points that corresponds to the round #
    (round 2 you'd get 2 points per kill etc.)
  • There are more zombies per round and they get faster as the rounds increase.
  • The game ends if your health reaches 0 or you kill all zombies in round 5,
    and if you win you get an extra 20 points.
  • (Tip: running around holding the shoot button seems to work pretty well.)
